Flight Simulator

Instructions:

Shift + 1 to Vary Left
Shift + 2 to Vary Right
Shift + 3 to Vary Top
Shift + 4 to Vary Bottom
Shift + 5 to Vary Near
Shift + 6 to Vary Far
W to Pitch Up
S to Pitch Down
A to Yaw Left
D to Yaw Right
Q to Roll Left
E to Roll Right
Up key to Increase Speed
Down to Decrease Speed
C to Switch Shading
Escape key to Quit
